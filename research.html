Note from the Lab

The pace of artificial intelligence development often outstrips our ability to fully comprehend the mechanisms at play. At Protoethik, we intentionally slow down the deployment phase to accelerate the understanding phase.

Our papers are not just reports; they are artifacts of a rigorous process designed to ensure that when we say a model is "thinking," we know exactly what that means.
