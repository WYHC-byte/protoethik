We are building the immune system of the intelligence age.

Protoethik was founded on a simple yet radical premise: as AI systems become more capable, they must become more thoughtful. We don't just build models that speak; we build models that reason.

Our Mission

Safety is the Axiom of Progress.

In the race for artificial general intelligence, speed often comes at the cost of reliability. At Protoethik, we reject this trade-off. We believe that true progress is impossible without a foundation of safety.

Our Story

Founded in January 2025.

Protoethik emerged at a critical inflection point in the history of AI. By late 2024, models had become incredibly fluent, yet they remained prone to confident hallucinations and opaque decision-making.

We started this company to fix the "black box" problem. Our team of researchers and engineers came together to build the ode-7 model family and TyloAI, proving that high-capability models can also be high-integrity partners.

How we work

Deep Thinking

We prioritize System 2 reasoning over System 1 reflex. Our models take the time to think, reducing errors and increasing nuance.

Transparent Reasoning

An answer is only as good as the logic behind it. We build tools like TyloAI that show their work, empowering users to verify facts.

Human Empowerment

We build tools like ode-code CLI to amplify human creativity, not replace it. We believe in a future where AI handles the drudgery.
