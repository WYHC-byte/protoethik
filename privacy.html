1. Introduction

At Protoethik ("we," "our," or "us"), our mission is to build AI systems that think deeply and reason transparently. We believe that transparency starts with how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our websites, products (including TyloAI and ode-code), and APIs (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and billing details when you register or subscribe.
  • User Content (Inputs): Data, text, code, or files you input into our models (e.g., "prompts") and the generated outputs.
  • Communications: Feedback, support queries, and other communications with us.

B. Information Automatically Collected

We collect standard technical data such as IP addresses, browser type, device information, and usage logs (tokens used, latency) to ensure system stability and security.

3. How We Use Data

We use your information primarily to provide, maintain, and improve our Services:

  • Processing your inputs to generate AI responses.
  • Managing your account and billing.
  • Detecting and preventing abuse (e.g., harmful content generation).
  • Debugging technical issues.

4. AI Model Training & Privacy

We distinguish between Consumer (Free/Plus) and Enterprise/API data handling to ensure professional compliance.

For Enterprise and API Users

We do NOT train on your data. Data submitted via our API or Enterprise plans is not used to train, retrain, or fine-tune our foundation models (such as the ode-7 family). Your proprietary code and reasoning chains remain yours.

For Consumer (Web Chat) Users

We may use de-identified, aggregated interactions from the free web chat interface to improve model safety and reasoning capabilities, unless you opt-out via settings. We strip personally identifiable information (PII) before any such use.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processors) who assist in operating our services, bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or other legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

6. Security & Retention

We implement enterprise-grade security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, or as needed to comply with our legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR for EU/UK, CCPA for California), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. You can manage most settings directly in your account dashboard or contact us for assistance.
